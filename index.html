PROCESS ENGINEERING

STEP 01: COMBUSTION

Sulfur Oxidation Furnace (F-101)

Molten sulfur is injected via high-pressure nozzles into a refractory-lined furnace. The atomized sulfur reacts with dehumidified air to generate a high-temperature stream of Sulfur Dioxide ($SO_2$).

S(l) + O₂(g) → SO₂(g) + Heat [ΔH = -297 kJ/mol]

Operational Temp: 1050°C | Material: High-Alumina Refractory Lining

STEP 02: CONVERSION

Catalytic Oxidation (R-201)

The 4-pass converter utilizes $V_2O_5$ catalyst beds. $SO_2$ gas is progressively converted to $SO_3$ with inter-stage cooling to maintain favorable thermodynamic equilibrium.

2SO₂(g) + O₂(g) ⇌ 2SO₃(g)

Converter efficiency is critical for meeting environmental emission standards.

STEP 03: ABSORPTION

Final Absorption Tower (T-301)

Unlike direct water mixing, $SO_3$ is absorbed into circulating 98% $H_2SO_4$. This prevents the formation of problematic acid mist and allows for controlled hydration.

SO₃ + H₂SO₄ → H₂S₂O₇ (Oleum)
H₂S₂O₇ + H₂O → 2H₂SO₄

SHE MANAGEMENT

Safety & Risk Mitigation

Amora Ventures operates under strict **Safety Integrity Level (SIL-3)** protocols. Sulfuric acid is highly corrosive and reacts violently with water; hence, operational discipline is paramount.

Personal Protection (PPE)

Level A hazmat suits for acid loading areas. Mandatory full-face respirators equipped with $SO_2$ cartridges.

Engineering Controls

Automated Emergency Shutdown Valves (ESV) and secondary containment bunds for all bulk storage tanks.
